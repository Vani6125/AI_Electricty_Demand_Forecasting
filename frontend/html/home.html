About Us

Who uses it?

Our AI-based electricity demand and peak load forecasting system is designed for power utilities, grid operators, energy planners, and policymakers. It also serves industries and large energy consumers seeking to optimize their energy usage and costs efficiently.

Why you should visit us?

Our system offers smarter energy solutions, cost reductions, and reliable grid management. With innovative technology tailored for Andhra Pradesh, we empower sustainable power distribution and help shape a more efficient energy future.

How we predict?

We leverage cutting-edge AI techniques, including machine learning and deep learning, alongside real-time inputs like historical energy consumption, weather conditions, and calendar events. This allows for highly accurate and dynamic forecasting.

Target and Goal

The target is to provide accurate electricity demand forecasts and identify peak load periods. The goal is to optimize grid operations, reduce energy wastage, improve reliability, and support sustainable energy planning for Andhra Pradesh's power system.
